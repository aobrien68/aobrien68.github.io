Bunker 19

Bunker 19 was bunker founded in Feburary 2019 by Comradrian troops during the Tem-Comrade war. I was built using the magic of duct-tape and run by Robot Stalin. It included three rooms, the mainroom, under staircase backroom, and cardboard bunker. It had a mail system, an alarm, vault, rations box, and trash can.

After the death of Robot Stalin to the Wuhan Virus the future of Bunker 19 was uncertain. However on April 8th Comradrian General Ember the Seven announced a renewal plan for the project under her supervision.

Although she said she would make policy changes to end the rights abuses, she stated "Mr. Hedgehog would not be unhanged". She further announced that left and right blahaj would manage the bunker and there are no plans to restore the cardboard bunker. She also announced reforms to Comradrian citizenship in the distupted territory where the bunker resides.
